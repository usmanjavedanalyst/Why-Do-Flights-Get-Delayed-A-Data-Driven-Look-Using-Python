# ✈️ Flight Delay Analysis: Data-Driven Aviation Insights

## 📌 Overview
A comprehensive aviation analytics project focused on identifying the **root causes of flight delays** using large-scale data. This analysis leverages Python to extract actionable insights across airlines, airports, and seasonal trends.

---

## 📊 Dataset
- **Total Records:** 5.5M+ flights  
- **Cleaned Dataset:** 5.48M records  
- **Data Loss:** ~1.7% (removed nulls & inconsistencies)  
- **Data Reliability:** 98.3% retention ensuring high-quality analysis  

---

## ⚙️ Tech Stack
- **Language:** Python  
- **Libraries:** Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn  
- **Techniques:** Data Cleaning, EDA, Correlation Analysis, Trend Analysis  

---

## 🔍 Key Metrics
- **Delayed Flights:** ~34% of total flights  
- **Average Departure Delay:** 9 minutes  
- **Average Arrival Delay:** 4 minutes  
- **Long-Haul Delay (2000+ miles):** ~11.3 minutes  

---

## 📈 Core Insights

### ✈️ Airline Performance
- **Highest Delays:**  
  - JetBlue (B6)  
  - Virgin America (VX)  
- Indication of **operational inefficiencies & scheduling gaps**

---

### 🏢 Airport Analysis
- **Highest Avg Delay Airport:** SWO → ~61 minutes  
- **Major Delay Hubs:** JFK, EWR, ORD  
- Congestion & traffic density significantly impact delays  

---

### 🌦️ Seasonal Trends
- **Peak Delay Periods:**  
  - Summer: June – July  
  - Winter: Dec – Jan  
- Strong correlation with **weather conditions & travel demand spikes**

---

### 🌍 Distance Impact
- **Long-haul flights (2000+ miles)** show the highest delays  
- Increased exposure to:
  - Weather disruptions  
  - Air traffic congestion  
  - Operational complexity  

---

## 🧠 Business Insights
- Identified **high-risk airlines and airports** impacting on-time performance  
- Revealed **seasonal delay patterns** for predictive planning  
- Highlighted **distance-based operational inefficiencies**  

---

## 💼 Business Impact
- Improved **flight scheduling strategies**  
- Enabled **better resource allocation** at busy airports  
- Enhanced **passenger experience** through delay prediction insights  

---

## 🚀 Conclusion
This project demonstrates how **large-scale data analysis (5.5M+ records)** can uncover hidden inefficiencies in aviation systems and drive **data-backed decision making** for improved operational performance.
